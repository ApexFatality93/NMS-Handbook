The No Man’s Sky Handbook is 100% free and always will be. There are no ads, no logins, and no paywalls.

All of the code is open source and publicly available. This makes it easy for others to contribute, learn from, or build upon.

How It Works

This site pulls data directly from No Man’s Sky game files. By parsing the actual MBIN/EXML files, it ensures the information is accurate and up to date.

Every recipe, stat, and item listed here is precisely what’s in the game. No guesswork, no outdated info.

Whenever the game receives an update, the new files can be uploaded and re-parsed to generate new data. This makes content after new updates quick, simple and reliable.
